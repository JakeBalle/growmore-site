5. Image Processing & OCR

Where users capture photographs of soil tests or agricultural documentation, GrowMore may use automated OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and cloud-based processing services to extract and interpret relevant information.

OCR extraction processes may contain inaccuracies.

Users remain responsible for independently verifying all extracted information before relying upon it.

Images captured for OCR processing are generally processed temporarily and are not intended to be permanently retained unless operationally necessary.

Uploaded soil test images and agricultural documentation may be temporarily uploaded to backend infrastructure and trusted third-party cloud-processing providers for the purposes of extracting and interpreting soil test information.

Original images are generally discarded following OCR processing and are not intended for long-term archival retention unless operationally required for support, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes.
